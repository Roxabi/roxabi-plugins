## Summary

Build `price.sh` — the single reachability-based version deriver — and rewire `/promote` so every pre-merge artifact (PR title, CHANGELOG heading, version file) becomes a **witness** of `price(BASE_SHA..M)`, never a source of it. Add the pre-merge CI guard (`release-consistency.yml`, reusable), its provisioner, and the version-file stamp step. All artifacts land in `roxabi-plugins`; the roxabi-live dogfood (run the provisioner there) is a follow-up.
